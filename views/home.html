* BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure used to assess if a person is at a healthy weight relative to their height. The BMI classification ranges are as follows:

BMI Classification
BMI < 18.5 Underweight
18.5 ≤ BMI < 24.9 Normal weight
25 ≤ BMI < 29.9 Overweight
30 ≤ BMI < 34.9 Obesity Class 1
35 ≤ BMI < 39.9 Obesity Class 2
BMI ≥ 40 Obesity Class 3 (morbid obesity)

* TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) represents the total amount of calories the body burns in a day, considering basal metabolism and physical activity.

Goal Daily Calories
Lose Weight TDEE - 500 kcal
Maintain Weight TDEE
Gain Weight TDEE + 500 kcal
